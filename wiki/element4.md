# Curio Knowledge Base: Instant Answers, Powered by AI

Finding the right answer should not mean scrolling through dozens of articles, guessing the exact keywords, or waiting hours for someone to reply. Curio's FAQ Knowledge Base and AI Chatbot exist to give users a single, intelligent entry point for finding trusted answers — fast.

What makes Curio's knowledge system unique is that it understands the **meaning** behind a question, answers only from information the platform actually trusts, and grows smarter every time the community solves a new problem.

Here is a simple, feature-first guide to how Curio helps users find answers.

---

## What Makes Curio Unique?

1. **Search That Understands You**: Curio doesn't just match keywords — it understands intent. Ask a question in your own words and you'll still get the right answer, even if your phrasing differs from the original article.
2. **A Chatbot You Can Trust**: The AI assistant never makes things up. Every answer is grounded in Curio's own FAQ or community knowledge, and it always shows you where the answer came from.
3. **Forum Search Only With Permission**: The FAQ is always checked first. Curio never quietly digs through community discussions — it asks for your consent before searching the forum.
4. **Knowledge That Grows Itself**: When the community solves a great question, admins can promote that solution into the official FAQ — so the knowledge base keeps expanding on its own.

---

## The Guided Tour of Knowledge Features

Here is how finding answers works in Curio, explained in simple language:

### 1. The Curated FAQ
The FAQ is Curio's central source of trusted answers. Administrators write and maintain articles covering common questions, policies, and procedures, neatly organized into categories. It's always the first place Curio looks when someone needs help.

### 2. Easy Category Browsing
Instead of one giant list, FAQ articles are grouped into clear categories that expand and collapse. Users can open a topic, preview the most relevant articles, and click "view all" to see the rest — reading answers right on the page without jumping between screens.

### 3. Intelligent Semantic Search
As a user types, Curio searches the FAQ by meaning, not just matching words. A search for "how do I get my money back" will surface the "Refund Policy" article even if those exact words never appear in it. The most relevant results rise to the top automatically.

### 4. The "Check the Forum?" Consent Step
If the FAQ doesn't have an answer, Curio doesn't silently search community discussions. Instead it asks: *"Not in the FAQ — do you want me to check the forum?"* Only when the user agrees does it search community questions and point them to the matching thread. Trusted, curated answers always come first.

### 5. The Conversational AI Chatbot
For users who would rather just ask, the floating chatbot offers a conversational way in. They type a question in plain language and the assistant finds the relevant knowledge and replies in a friendly, easy-to-read format — no category browsing required.

### 6. Grounded, Trustworthy Answers
The chatbot answers in three layers, in order. First it tries the curated FAQ. If it finds nothing confident there, it asks permission to check resolved community discussions. If neither has a good match, it honestly says so and suggests browsing the FAQ or raising a new question — rather than inventing an answer.

### 7. Always-Cited Sources
Every chatbot answer shows its source — whether it came from a specific FAQ article or a community thread — with a link to follow. This transparency lets users verify answers themselves and builds trust in everything the assistant says.

### 8. Promoting Community Answers into the FAQ
Great answers shouldn't stay buried in a forum thread. Once a community question is resolved, an admin can promote it — question and accepted answer — straight into the official FAQ, complete with a "Promoted from Q&A" badge. A one-off solution becomes permanent, searchable knowledge.

### 9. The FAQ Duplicate Guard
To keep the knowledge base clean, Curio warns admins before they create an article that's too similar to one that already exists. If a near-duplicate is detected, the admin is shown the existing entry and can either rethink it or deliberately override the guard — preventing the FAQ from filling up with redundant articles.

### 10. Works Even Without an AI Key
Curio's intelligence layer has a built-in offline mode. With no AI key configured, search, duplicate detection, and the chatbot all keep working using deterministic, on-device logic. This means the platform runs fully in development, testing, and demos with zero cost and zero network calls.

## A Knowledge System That Improves Over Time

The real power of Curio's knowledge base is the cycle it creates: users ask questions, the community contributes solutions, the best of those solutions are preserved into the FAQ, and future users find them instantly.

Over time the knowledge base becomes richer, search becomes sharper, and people spend less time looking for answers. Curated content, community expertise, and AI-powered discovery come together into one experience that quietly gets better every day.
